I got my first and only tattoo on the night I turned 18. I had gotten the image off of a google search of the word "glyph" because my friend had suggested it (window 1). At the time, I really liked it because it was simple and the pinterest chart told me it meant content. My friends all used to call it my barcode (window 2). I think it looks more like actual content on a page (window 3). Looking back at it, I actually think that is what it was supposed to mean in the first place. Now, over time, the lines have kind of bled together. I wish it looked like a cool wave pattern or something (window 4). Instead, it's just turning into a block (window 5).

And the future may hold some unexpected changes (window 6).

Such is life.
